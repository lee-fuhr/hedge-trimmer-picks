Best hedge trimmers for 2026

Quick answer

The DEWALT 20V Hedge Trimmer is our top pick for its powerful brushless motor and excellent build quality, making it the best overall cordless hedge trimmer for most homeowners.

The Makita 18V LXT is the runner-up for pros who value smooth operation and balance, while the Ryobi ONE+ is the budget champion and the Bosch 36V delivers premium performance for large gardens.

Cordless hedge trimmers free you from extension cords and gas fumes, but choosing one means balancing battery power, blade length, weight, and vibration control. Most trimmers in this class have dual-action blades that reduce kickback, and the battery voltage often dictates how thick a branch they can handle. For small, shaped hedges, a lightweight trimmer with a shorter blade is enough; for long, unruly rows of overgrown shrubs, a higher-voltage model with a longer blade saves time. Pay close attention to the battery system, if you already own tools from a platform like Ryobi One+ or Makita LXT, buying a bare tool is far more affordable than a full kit.
